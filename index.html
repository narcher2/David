David the Shepard

  • High Concept: Collect/herd as many sheep as you can into safety before they get eaten by wolves.
  • Player Experience Goals: Strategy, time management, finesse.
  • Genre: Collect-fest.
  • Game play: Find your sheep and protect them from wolves. Use the arrow keys to move and click to use your sling.
  • Prototype goal: Collision detection, retro graphics, trying new things.
  • Features: Collecting, calculation-based combat.
  • Setting/Premise: The range.
  • Story: It's just another day in the life of a shepard, your sheep got released from their enclosure and now are threatened by the surrounding wolves. You have nothing but your sling and shepard's staff to ensure their safety.
  • Target Audience: Casuals.
  • Play time: Varies
  • Strategies: Gathering sheep closest to the enclosure first to ensure saving the most sheep possible.
  • Prototype Assets: I drew everything myself, got sounds and music from the interweb..
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: That one quest in RuneScape where you need to herd sheep.
